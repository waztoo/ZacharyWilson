Hello! I am Zachary Wilson. I am a 2nd year mechanical engineering student attending Rochester Institute of Technology, specializing in energy and the environment. In my free time, I enjoy designing and building things, competing in trivia, creative writing, often for running D&D or other tabletop roleplaying games, cooking and baking, model kits, playing video games, exercising, spider watching, and researching various topics. In 2025, I graduated from Park View High School and the engineering program of the Loudoun Academy of Engineering and Technology summa cum laude. As an avid environmentalist, I wanted to find somewhere where I could gain relevant experience in the field of green energy, which, along with the great co-op opportunities, led me to attend RIT. Since 2016, I have done work for and volunteered for Glen Echo Park Aquarium, including teaching various guests about the exhibits, desinging the initial aquarium website, assisting with accounting work and financial organization via excel, and designing products to be used (see personal projects). Additionally, I have done volunteer work with the Sterling Foundation and have membership in the Loudoun Climate Project and the Alliance for the Chesapeake Bay. For a little over a year, I have been working part time as a delivery expert at Domino's Pizza, wherein I gained significant experience in working in a fast-paced, team based environment. I am currently searching for co-op and internship opportunities for the summer of 2027 and beyond.
